About Us

We plan nutritious meals for illness survivors.

Gluu.! believes in the importance of healthy living.
Founded in 2020, Gluu.! members have devoted their lives to serve illness survivors in need of a change in their living quality, specifically through the betterment of their daily consumptions. Gluu.! is set to become the pioneer in the youth-led diet plan service business, designed to aid patients with chronic illnesses in figuring out beneficial and nutritious consumptions for their health and longevity.
