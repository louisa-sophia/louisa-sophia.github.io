About Us

We plan nutritious meals for warriors.

Gluu.! believes in the importance of healthy living.
Founded in 2020, Gluu.! members have devoted their lives to serve warriors of chronic illnesses whom are in need of a change in their living quality, specifically through the betterment of their daily consumptions. Gluu.! is set to become the pioneer in the youth-led diet plan service business, designed to aid warriors in figuring out beneficial and nutritious consumptions for their health and longevity.

Our Services


Gluu. currently provides three different meal plan options, each crafted to serve the needs of different people. Made in honor of the world's most influential physicians, CoriMeals Diabetic (in honor of Nobel Prize awardee Gerty Cori, MD), HausMeals Carcinoma (in honor of Nobel Prize awardee Harald zur Hausen) and FitEats Nutrition by Gluu. is here to serve you.
